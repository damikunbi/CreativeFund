# CreativeFund

A decentralized creative project funding platform that incentivizes innovative artistic endeavors and rewards consistent creative output through blockchain-based credit systems.

## Overview

CreativeFund is a smart contract platform that enables creators to track their projects and earn credits based on their creative innovation. The system includes features for tracking creative innovation levels, investing credits for long-term artistic goals, and managing a sustainable creative economy.

## Features

- **Project Tracking**: Record and validate creative projects and artistic endeavors
- **Innovation Bonuses**: Reward creators who maintain consistent innovative output
- **Credit Redemption**: Allow creators to redeem earned creative credits
- **Credit Investment**: Enable creators to invest credits for long-term creative ventures
- **Creative Statistics**: Track overall project metrics and credit distribution

## Core Functions

- `launch-creative-project`: Begin tracking a new creative project
- `complete-creative-project`: Finalize a project and receive creative credits
- `claim-creative-funding`: Exchange accumulated credits for creative funding
- `invest-creative-credits`: Invest credits for long-term creative ventures
- `liquidate-invested-credits`: Retrieve invested credits (with potential penalties for early liquidation)

## Technical Details

- Maximum creative fund capacity: 3,000,000 credits
- Base project reward: 20 credits
- Innovation bonus: 5 credits per level (up to 6 levels)
- Minimum investment duration: 864 blocks (approximately 6 days)
- Early liquidation penalty: 20%

## Getting Started

1. Deploy the contract to your blockchain
2. Creators can start tracking their creative projects
3. Complete projects to earn creative credits
4. Invest credits for long-term creative goals
5. Redeem credits for creative funding when ready

## Security

The system includes safeguards to prevent exceeding the creative fund capacity and ensures that only legitimate projects are rewarded.
